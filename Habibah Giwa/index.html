My Personal Mission

My personal mission is to live a life of impact; to make a positive impact on every girl child, women and the vulnerable.

How to achieved it

I used to think and believe that Tech is made for men only,thus having a low foresight about it. However as a female and Tech enthusiast and practitioner,I believe with hardwork and due diligence,great reward can be achieved.I therefore intend to encourage females around me to test his water while I guide the to success.My core values are deeply rooted in a strengthened bond.This foundation is a great asset for me to create a synergy of young women for the Tech world. Although I have a set plan for a lucrative and impactful venture,I do not currently run an active enterprise.Be in gapart of this training will equipme with needed skills and abilities to bring to life my venture-in-progress. In my community(hometown),with the level of their socialization,
I realized the absence of Techhub and insufficient Tech enthusiast despite the current flood wave of technology.I fear that they might be leftout in future and my goal is to prevent such from happening by filling the gap. This venture shall promote innovation for technology and serve as an arena where people can come share ideas,chill,learn,and seal deals.An avenue where we can collaborate with organisations or schools to help with whatever project they want to build technologically.A place of course with 24hours of power supply and strong internet,among other facilities.

In conclusion

We shall conduct outreach,provide support and render help to people in my community and beyond.I believe this goal will leads to empowerment.And when there arejobs and empowerment in a community,it will enhance the alleviation of poverty.
